Medical Reference Modules
==========================

Medical Specialties
-------------------

Find and understand medical specialties and specialists.

Pydantic Models
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **SpecialtyCategory**: Organization of medical specialties
- **Subspecialty**: Sub-fields within a specialty
- **MedicalSpecialist**: Complete specialist information
- **MedicalSpecialistDatabase**: Database with query methods

Key Methods
~~~~~~~~~~~

- ``get_by_category()``: Find all specialists in a category
- ``search_by_condition()``: Find specialists who treat a condition
- ``get_surgical_specialists()``: Get all surgical specialties
- ``get_all_categories()``: List all available categories

Disease Information
-------------------

Generate comprehensive disease information and patient education materials.

Coverage Areas
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Disease identity and classification
- Disease background and pathophysiology
- Epidemiology and prevalence
- Risk factors and etiology
- Diagnostic criteria
- Treatment options
- Clinical presentation
- Research and advancements
- Special populations (children, elderly, pregnancy)
- Living with the disease
