Drug Information Modules
=========================

RxNorm Database Client
----------------------

Access standardized drug information from the RxNorm database maintained by the U.S. National Library of Medicine.

Key Features
~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Get RxCUI (drug identifier) from drug names
- Retrieve comprehensive drug properties
- Handle misspelled or variant drug names
- Validate drugs in the database
- No API key required (public access)

Medicine Information
--------------------

Get comprehensive medicine information including:

- Primary uses and therapeutic benefits
- Dosage guidelines (pediatric, adult, geriatric)
- Side effects and adverse reactions
- Interactions with other drugs
- Storage and administration instructions
- Contraindications and precautions

Drug Interactions
-----------------

Analyze interactions between drugs and with diseases or food.

Drug-Drug Interactions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Check for dangerous combinations of medications.

Drug-Disease Interactions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Understand how patient conditions affect drug therapy:

- Efficacy impact (enhanced or reduced effectiveness)
- Safety impact (increased side effects or toxicity)
- Dosage adjustments needed
- Monitoring requirements
- Contraindications

Drug-Food Interactions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Check for food-drug interactions and dietary restrictions.

Drug Comparison
---------------

Compare similar drugs and their properties.
